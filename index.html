DrugFX — AI Drug Information System

Featured

An AI-powered drug intelligence platform that delivers instant, context-aware information about medicines — usage, dosage, side effects, precautions, and interactions. Supports both text-based queries (drug name) and image-based input (medicine strip or bottle), using OCR to extract drug names from real-world photos. The backend combines NLP and LLMs through a RAG pipeline grounded in a structured pharmaceutical knowledge base — delivering reliable, hallucination-resistant responses. Designed for patients, caregivers, and healthcare students who need trustworthy drug data fast.
